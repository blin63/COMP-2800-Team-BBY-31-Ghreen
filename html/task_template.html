Task Description:

Replace meat in at least 1 meal with any of the following: legumes, nuts, seeds, beans, tofu.

As your speed increases, every 10km/hr above 90 km/hr results in increases in fuel consumption of approximately 10.

Further Information

One study of 55,504 people found that those who ate medium amounts of meat per day (1.8–3.5 ounces (50–100 grams)) had a significantly lower carbon footprint than those who ate more than 3.5 ounces (100 grams) per day. Eating more plant-based protein can dramatically cut your greenhouse gas emissions. For reference, a serving of meat is around 3 ounces (85 grams)
